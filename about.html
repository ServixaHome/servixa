Who We Are

Navi Mumbai's Leading Repair Service

Servixa Home was founded with a simple mission — to provide Navi Mumbai's residents with honest, fast, and affordable home appliance repair. We noticed that people were often left frustrated by unreliable technicians, hidden charges, and poor service quality.

We set out to change that. With certified technicians, transparent pricing, and a 30-day service warranty, Servixa Home has become one of Navi Mumbai's most trusted appliance repair services.

Our motto, सेवा • सुरक्षा • समृद्धि (Service, Safety, Prosperity), guides everything we do. We believe that a great service experience at home leads to greater peace of mind and prosperity for every family.
