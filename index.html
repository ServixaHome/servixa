FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We have answers. Find quick answers to common questions about our AC and washing machine services.

What are AC service charges in Navi Mumbai? ▼
Our AC service charges start at just ₹299 for basic repairs. Deep cleaning starts at ₹499, and installation charges start at ₹799. We believe in transparent pricing and will provide a complete estimate after inspection before any work begins.
What is the cost of AC gas filling? ▼
AC gas filling charges start at ₹899, which includes both the refrigerant gas and labor. The final price depends on your AC type (Split, Window, or Inverter) and the quantity/type of gas required.
Do you provide same day AC repair? ▼
Yes! We provide same-day AC repair, installation, and gas filling services across Navi Mumbai. For bookings made before 12:00 PM, our technician will be at your doorstep on the same day.
What are washing machine repair charges? ▼
Washing machine repair charges start at ₹299. The final cost depends on the issue (e.g., motor replacement, drum repair, or water leakage fix) and any spare parts required. Our technician will provide a clear quote before starting.
Which areas do you serve in Navi Mumbai? ▼
We aggressively serve Ghansoli and Kopar Khairane. We also offer reliable home repair services in Vashi, Airoli, Nerul, Belapur, Seawoods, and Kharghar.
