FAQ

Common Questions

How long does AC repair take? ▼
Most AC repairs take 1–3 hours depending on the issue. Gas filling takes about 1 hour. Deep cleaning takes 45–60 minutes per unit.
Do you carry spare parts? ▼
Our technicians carry common spare parts. For specific or rare parts, we may need 1–2 days. We'll inform you in advance.
Is there a service charge for the visit? ▼
We offer free inspection on every visit. The service charge is only for the actual repair work done. No hidden visit charges.
Do you provide warranty on repairs? ▼
Yes! All our AC repairs come with a service warranty. If the same issue reoccurs within the warranty period, we'll fix it free of charge.
How often should I get my AC serviced? ▼
We recommend AC servicing at least once a year, ideally before summer (March-April). Regular servicing improves cooling efficiency and extends AC lifespan.
