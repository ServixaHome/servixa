Our Comprehensive Range of AC Services in Ghansoli

Servixa Home is a one-stop-shop for all your cooling requirements. Our specialized service suite includes:

1. Split and Window AC Repair

Is your Split AC blowing warm air or is your Window AC refusing to turn on? We troubleshoot all mechanical and electronic issues, including PCB repair, fan motor replacement, condenser coil fixes, capacitor replacements, and thermostat adjustments.

2. Jet Pump Deep Cleaning & Servicing

Dust and dirt build-up inside the AC coils can block airflow and significantly reduce cooling efficiency. Our comprehensive jet pump servicing washes away stubborn dirt from the evaporator coil, blower, condenser coil, and drain tray, restoring original cooling power and reducing power consumption.

3. AC Gas Filling / Refilling

Low refrigerant levels due to minor leakages are a common cause of poor cooling. Our technicians perform a thorough leak detection test, repair any punctures in the copper coils, vacuum the system, and refill high-grade refrigerant gas (R32, R410, R22) to the correct pressure levels.

4. AC Installation and Shifting

Moving to a new home in Ghansoli? We offer neat and professional split and window AC installation and uninstallation services. We ensure proper gas piping, electrical connections, and outdoor unit placement to guarantee optimal cooling and avoid future water leakage issues.
