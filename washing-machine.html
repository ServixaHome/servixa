FAQ

Common Questions

How long does washing machine repair take? ▼
Most repairs are completed in 1–3 hours. Motor replacement or drum repair may take 2–4 hours. If we need to order a specific part, it may take 1–2 extra days.
Is it better to repair or replace an old machine? ▼
If repair cost is less than 50% of a new machine's price, repair is usually the better option. Our technician will honestly advise you after inspection.
Do you carry genuine spare parts? ▼
We use high-quality compatible parts. For genuine OEM parts, we can source them for you, which may take 1–2 additional days.
Is there warranty on the repair? ▼
Yes! All repairs come with a service warranty. If the same problem recurs within the warranty period, we'll fix it at no extra charge.
